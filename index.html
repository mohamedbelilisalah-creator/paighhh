Early 2026 — Foundation
Brand Identity System
Built the full PAIGH brand identity: color palette (Cream, Jet, Sunny + secondaries), font pairing (Quincy CF display + Brandon Grotesque body), visual system "Verspielte Rahmen" — arched/circle cutouts on solid color blocks with hand-drawn wave elements. Documented all hard rules (no AI visuals, no berry/magenta/neon).

Defined Clara (Conscious Boho), Mara (Comfort-First), Sofia (Expressive Inclusive), and Lena (Story-Led). Each persona was mapped to content formats, hooks, objections, and channel preferences. Mara became the photorealistic AI character anchor for video content.

Feb–Mar 2026 — Content Strategy
Content Playbook + 10 Repeatable Series
Built the full content engine: 6 content pillars, 10 repeatable series (Meet the Maker, Comfort Test, One Piece Many Ways, Print Quiz, Fits Real Bodies, Real Cost of Fair, POV/Clothes Could Talk, Anti-Haul, Behind the Print, PAIGH Family UGC), posting cadence of 5 posts/week at 7–9PM CET.

Mar 2026 — Data

Root cause diagnosis
All failures lead with the brand's argument rather than the viewer's feeling. The audience doesn't engage with "here's why to care" — they engage with "here's exactly how this feels." Every failing post forgot to put the person before the product.

Upcoming Content — Why They Fit

Ghibli & Urlaub: the brief.

Two concepts already in development. Here's the strategic case for each — anchored in performance data, brand fit, and persona match.

Series 01 — Illustrated / AI-Assisted
Ghibli-World PAIGH
A soft, illustrated aesthetic — Ghibli's warm countryside light, gentle movement, and human-scale world — applied to PAIGH's garments and the slow-fashion life. The characters wear PAIGH; the world feels handmade.
  • ✓Ghibli's visual language — golden warmth, natural textures, flowing fabric — is a direct match for PAIGH's existing palette and "Verspielte Rahmen" system.
  • ✓Seasonal feeling content (eng 4.22) is the top performer. Ghibli reels are seasonal feeling expressed as world-building — same emotional trigger, richer visual.
  • ✓Clothing shown on characters in motion, not on hangers. Solves the "product-first" failure mode — the character and world come first, the garment is incidental.
  • ✓Highly shareable — Ghibli aesthetics have near-universal recognition and cross-cultural appeal across DACH markets.
  • ✓Works as still illustration → reel animation → Pinterest static. Three platforms, one asset.
Clara — Dreamy boho world Sofia — Colorful & expressive Lena — Handmade, fair-world symbolism
Series 02 — Real-life / Mara-led
Urlaub mit PAIGH
Travel content built around Mara — the photorealistic AI character. One PAIGH piece, multiple destinations. "Sofa. Fahrrad. Strand. Eine Hose." The format captures PAIGH's everyday-versatility promise in its most appealing setting: summer freedom.
  • ✓Directly executes the highest-potential untested idea from the Metricool Sprint: "Sofa. Fahrrad. Strand. Eine Hose." — built for reach + saves.
  • ✓Travel triggers the emotional "Fernweh" feeling — a proven entry point for German-language audiences in summer (Jun–Aug peak).
  • ✓Mara's character is purpose-built for comfort-test and movement content. Urlaub is the natural extension: one piece, many real-life situations.
  • ✓The "Pack with me" and "1 piece, many occasions" formats sit inside the top save-driving series in the playbook. Urlaub fuses both.
  • ✓Comfort + mobility message reaches Mara persona directly — busy woman, real life, real trips. No aspirational gap to bridge.
Mara — Comfort & mobility Clara — Intentional travel packing Sofia — Summer expression
